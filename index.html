    A business with more than five major goals won’t be able to focus enough resources on any one of them to accomplish it well. To make your business more efficient, pick only three to five goals. Make them specific, measurable, achievable, relevant (as in they would have clear impact on improving your business), and time-bound – that is associated with a deadline. Finally, put them in order of priority, because it’s efficient to focus on some goals before others, rather than trying to do all of them at once. Some are more urgent, and some can only be achieved with a methodical, long-term plan.
